About

What you should include on an "About" page:

  • About us is an important page.
  • Show your costumers that they can trust you.
  • Be truthful and open.
  • Give an insight into the history of the company.
  • Describe why the company is good at what they do.
  • Describe which customers the company has had, and what you have helped them with.
  • Write feedback you have received from customers.
  • Attach photos of employees and their work areas.
  • Add contact information.
